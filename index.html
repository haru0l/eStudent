Sekolah Kebangsaan Kota Raja or its short name SK Kota Raja, is a national school located in Panchor.
In 2009, Sekolah Kebangsaan Kota Raja had 104 male students and 98 female students, bringing the total number of students to 202. It has a total of 26 teachers.
